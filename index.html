About us

CARL (Cognitive Applications Reseach Lab) is the student-run Research and Development Lab of SRM IST. CARL offers you a platform to enhance and nurture your research acumen and development abilities.
By bridging the gap between students and academia, we not only make research easily accessible for everyone but also hope to encourage students to write their own papers.
